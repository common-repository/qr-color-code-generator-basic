== Description ==

Everywhere you look you see QR Codes these days. With the mobile device market growing at a fast pace, QR Codes are becoming very important marketing tools. With a scan of a mobile phone or tablet, they can direct people to your website, the location of your business, etc. This generator will allow you to create QR Codes and give them their own identity.

*   Custom Admin Panel
*   Custom QR Color Code Widget

== Installation ==

1.  Go to the dashboard of your WordPress site and click "Add New" in the "Plugins" panel. From there you would click "Upload", browse to QR-Color-Code-Generator-Basic.zip, and click "Install Now."
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. You will generate QR codes in the "Create QR Codes" panel, where you have 6 different options. After clicking "Generate" your QR code will appear, and you can download it in PDF or PNG format by clicking the appropriate button.
4. You can display your QR Codes in the custom widget that comes with this plugin. You can choose a title, description, and which code to display in the widget.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Why won't my codes appear after clicking "Generate"? =

Make sure you have write access enabled on the directory of this plugin and it's subdirectories.
